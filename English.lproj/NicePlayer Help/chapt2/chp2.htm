DVD Playback

DVD Playback support is still under development, but here are some short instructions:

Drag the dvd icon on to the NicePlayer Icon to open a DVD (or use the open panel and choose the dvd disc to open).

Controls
⇧⌘←Previous chapter.
⇧⌘→Next chapter.
⇧←, ⇧↑, ⇧→, ⇧↓DVD navigation.
⇧↩Enter on DVD player.

Mouse navigation and click also work.

⇧M will take you back to the main menu.
