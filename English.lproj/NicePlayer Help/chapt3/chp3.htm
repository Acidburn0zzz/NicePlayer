Playing DivX/WMV

To be able to playback most content in NicePlayer, you recommend installing the following plugins:

  • The WMV Codec for QuickTime to open Windows media files
    (http://www.microsoft.com/windows/windowsmedia/player/wmcomponents.mspx)
  • XiphQT to open OGG files and Codecs
    (http://xiph.org/quicktime/)
  • The Perian Codec for QuickTime to open everything else
    (http://perian.org/)
